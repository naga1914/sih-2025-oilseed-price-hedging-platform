## 🚀 About The Project

Krushi Portal is an AI-powered and blockchain-enabled platform designed to help oilseed farmers manage market price volatility through virtual hedging, price forecasting, secure digital contracts, and real-time market intelligence.

The platform provides a transparent ecosystem connecting farmers, FPOs, buyers, and agricultural stakeholders while promoting financial literacy and informed decision-making.

Our solution addresses the challenges faced by oilseed farmers who often suffer losses due to unpredictable market fluctuations and lack access to modern risk management tools.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Reduce price-related risks for oilseed farmers.
* Provide AI-driven market insights and price forecasting.
* Enable secure digital forward contracts.
